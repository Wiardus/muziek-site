About Me

My name is Ynte Wiardus Braaksma.
I am from Marrum, The Netherlands and I am studying Communication and Multimedia Design at NHL Stenden in Leeuwarden.
I am specialized in music composition and production for media such as games, videos and movies.
I also play guitar in the band Levikta and guitar, bass and keyboard for various musical solo projects.
